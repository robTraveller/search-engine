Welcome to Aurora Team Wiki Space.

About Us

SAP Business OneAurora team is a development team focusing on all the reporting and analytics topics for SAP Business One products. Our mission is to extend the functionalites of B1 main application with various add-ons, platforms, utilities and tools, which could give our ISV/SSP/customers/supports more benefits throughout the whole prodcut life cycle.
